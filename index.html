Authentic Korean Food on Thayer

Soban Korean Eatery is a family owned restaurant that specializes in authentic Korean cuisine. Everything on our menu is handmade in our kitchen. All of our marinating sauces and side dishes are made in the house and served to our customers. Our fresh food and delicious menu brings our customers back again and again.

Soban + b.b.q. Chicken

Since we offer a wide variety of Asian food, you are bound to find something you will love. From all-time favorites like bibimbap to delicious side dishes, you will not be disappointed. Our wide variety of food marinated meats will make your taste buds happy. In a hurry? We will assist you with takeout services. Just give us a call!
